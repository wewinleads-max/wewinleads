Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see initial qualified leads within 30 days. Strategic ads can produce results within days, while SEO typically shows significant improvement within 60-90 days. Our AI outreach begins generating responses within the first week.

What makes your approach different from other marketing agencies?

We specialize exclusively in B2B commercial contracts and understand the unique challenges of Brisbane to Gold Coast businesses. Our 2-stage system combines immediate results (ads & outreach) with long-term growth (SEO), and we only work with businesses serious about growth.

Do you work with businesses outside Brisbane and Gold Coast?

While our expertise is in the Brisbane to Gold Coast corridor, we can work with Queensland businesses outside this area. Our local market knowledge gives us a significant advantage in targeting and messaging.

What if I'm not ready to commit to a full program?

We offer flexible pricing options including performance-based models where you only pay when we deliver results. We can also start with a single service (like AI outreach) and expand based on performance.

How do you ensure the leads are qualified?

Our targeting is laser-focused on your ideal customer profile. We target specific job titles, company sizes, industries, and geographic areas. Plus, our messaging pre-qualifies prospects before they even respond.

What kind of reporting will I receive?

You'll receive detailed monthly reports covering all key metrics: lead volume, lead quality, conversion rates, cost per lead, and ROI. We also provide real-time access to dashboards so you can track progress daily.
